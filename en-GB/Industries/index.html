Industries

Our industry knowledge covers such industries as construction, logistics and property maintenance.

All our clients have the benefit of our detailed industry knowledge within the construction sector. With a pool of applicants, we support our clients with employees who have the skills needed to get the job done right – ranging from general labor to skilled machine operators.

We know the challenges associated with construction projects: time, skill, budget and delivery. That inside information enabled us to streamline the process and deliver exactly what our clients need — working within budget to deliver skilled specialists as quickly as possible.

For job seekers, Base is committed to being the best employer in the industry for construction workers. We offer our employees the security of permanent and temporary employment and a diversified work experience through our many and varied clients.

We want to be a long-term partner for both the employer and the employee – throughout our careers.

Let’s grow business and develop careers together.

Base is a strong partner in human resources for industrial manufacturing.
